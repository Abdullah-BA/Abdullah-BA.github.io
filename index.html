Philosophy & Process

My approach to quant research centers on structural signal validation, Bayesian falsifiability, and adaptive frameworks. Good signals don’t just explain — they anticipate, fail gracefully, and recompose under new regimes. I reject model overfitting, sentiment overlays, and unverified edge.
